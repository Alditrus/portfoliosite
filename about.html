I'm Jamison Orten

I am a User Experience and User Interface Designer with an interest in immersive design. I am working towards a career in creating spatial experiences in both physical and virtual environments. To build toward that goal, I'm developing a strong foundation in UX/UI design to understand how users interact with interfaces, how to research and validate design decisions, and how to create intuitive experiences.

I've developed proficiency in Figma and Adobe Illustrator through projects like designing a communications dashboard for decentralized autonomous organizations where I focused on reducing information overload and creating social spaces to drive greater coordination. I'm also skilled in AI-assisted coding which helps me understand technical constraints and communicate effectively with developers. I am currently seeking internship opportunities where I can contribute to meaningful projects while continuing to develop my UX practice in a collaborative team environment.
